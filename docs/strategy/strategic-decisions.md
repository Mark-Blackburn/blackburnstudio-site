## Status meanings used here

- `Proposed`: recommended direction, not yet confirmed
- `Needs discussion`: active decision area requiring owner review
- `Deferred`: important but not yet ready for resolution
- `Approved`: confirmed direction
- `Rejected`: considered and not being pursued

## Decisions requiring owner review

- Remaining owner-review topics are now primarily wording and evidence questions rather than launch-direction questions:
- exact public biography wording
- final case-study selection and publication permissions
- detailed service boundaries in live copy
- support operating capacity and response model
- final service-area wording
- discovery scope, pricing, and deliverable structure
- discovery and implementation proposal boundaries in contracts
- intellectual-property and deliverable-usage treatment pending legal review
