# Target Clients

Status: Provisional

Last reviewed: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

This document defines provisional Digital client groups for Blackburn Studio. It does not replace the current photography audience described in [../current-state/site-overview.md](../current-state/site-overview.md).

It should be read with [brand-architecture.md](brand-architecture.md) and [digital-services-strategy.md](digital-services-strategy.md).

Working context:

- Approved working direction: Blackburn Studio remains the umbrella brand.
- Approved working direction: Photography and Digital are parallel disciplines.
- Approved working direction: Digital launch focus is websites, with deeper capabilities presented as supporting depth.
- Approved working direction: Workflow and process improvement is a core Digital capability.

## Primary website clients

### Small and medium local businesses

Common problems:

- outdated websites
- unclear service presentation
- poor mobile usability
- inconsistent enquiries or unclear next steps

What they value:

- clarity
- professional presentation
- practical guidance
- a site that is manageable after launch

Likely project size and complexity:

- generally straightforward to moderate website work

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- considered website design and implementation
- calm, practical communication
- potential integration of original photography where relevant

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting large-agency turnaround capacity
- expecting broad marketing-services bundling by default

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of clearer service-led websites
- examples of site refreshes replacing weak existing presences

### Trades and service businesses

Common problems:

- websites that do not explain services clearly
- weak trust signals
- poor enquiry pathways
- low-quality or generic imagery

What they value:

- straightforward process
- clear outcomes
- credibility with local customers

Likely project size and complexity:

- generally practical website projects with focused service content needs

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- clean service-site structure
- practical calls to action
- photography capability that can support stronger real-world presentation

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting broad outsourced marketing operations
- expecting bargain-volume production models

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of service-business sites with improved clarity and trust

### Independent professionals

Common problems:

- personal sites that undersell credibility
- weak portfolio or service presentation
- fragmented online presence

What they value:

- professionalism
- clarity
- presentation aligned with reputation
- thoughtful handling of personal brand

Likely project size and complexity:

- generally small to moderate projects with high importance placed on presentation quality

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- experience-led, personal delivery model
- strong alignment between visual tone and service presentation

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting a mass-content production approach
- expecting a generic template process with no input required

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of portfolio or professional-presence websites

### Creative businesses

Common problems:

- websites that fail to present work effectively
- tension between aesthetics and usability
- weak content structure

What they value:

- visual judgement
- curation
- sensible interaction design
- respect for the work itself

Likely project size and complexity:

- small to moderate website projects, sometimes with higher design sensitivity

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- current photography site already demonstrates strong visual restraint and curation
- digital delivery can be framed as an extension of that sensibility

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting a broad branding agency engagement when only digital delivery is offered

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples where presentation quality and usability both matter

What they value:

- reliability

Likely project size and complexity:

- moderate website work, occasionally extending into portal or administration needs

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting a large support desk or agency resource model

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of community or membership-oriented website improvements

Common problems:

- lack of trust in previous digital setup

What they value:

- simplification
- reliable delivery
- a site that does not become a burden

Likely project size and complexity:

- small to moderate website rebuild or redevelopment projects

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

Unsuitable client expectations:

Useful evidence or case studies:

## Custom digital solution clients

Cross-client workflow problems frequently observed:

- work still completed on paper
- repeated re-keying of information
- processes dependent on one person's knowledge
- information passed manually between people
- customers calling for tasks that could be self-service
- approval and follow-up processes managed through long email chains
- spreadsheets being used as informal operational systems
- owners who know something is inefficient but cannot define the solution
- organisations that have accepted inefficient processes because "that is how it has always been done"

Cross-client value sought:

- an external view of how the work operates
- help identifying the real problem
- practical prioritisation
- simpler processes before new technology is introduced
- staged improvements that do not overwhelm the organisation
- direct involvement from an experienced practitioner

Important clarification:

- Blackburn Studio can sometimes identify useful enhancements the owner was not originally considering.
- Not every client requires a custom platform; in some cases a simpler process change or staged workflow improvement is the better fit.

### Organisations with manual administrative processes

Common problems:

- repetitive admin
- duplicated data entry
- approval bottlenecks
- fragmented records
- paper forms and manual records that delay work
- spreadsheet and email chains acting as fragile operational systems

What they value:

- efficiency
- reliability
- reduced manual effort
- practical systems that staff will actually use
- clearer understanding of root operational issues before implementation
- staged improvement plans that can be adopted progressively

Likely project size and complexity:

- moderate complexity, often beyond a standard website but still tightly scoped

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- workflow and process discovery can identify bottlenecks and unnecessary handling before technology decisions are made
- practical digital implementation can then be staged based on value, effort, and operational risk

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting enterprise consulting capacity or major transformation programmes

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of workflow reduction, reduced duplication, or clearer operational handling

### Clubs, leagues, and member-based organisations

Common problems:

- member information spread across spreadsheets and email
- volunteer admin overload
- poor access to structured member content
- paper-based forms and approvals slowing member administration
- operational steps reliant on a single person's knowledge

What they value:

- trusted ongoing relationship
- practical member-facing tools
- systems that reduce administrative overhead
- help identifying improvement opportunities they may not have previously recognised

Likely project size and complexity:

- moderate website-plus-system work or portal-style work

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- the supplied direction explicitly includes portals and deeper technical capability

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting a large product team or broad enterprise procurement model

Useful evidence or case studies:

- member portal, administrative dashboard, or workflow examples

### Businesses requiring client or staff portals

Common problems:

- client communication spread across email
- no structured self-service area
- internal process friction
- repeated manual transfer of information between people and tools

What they value:

- access clarity
- reduced back-and-forth
- systems that support service delivery
- practical diagnosis of underlying workflow issues before committing to a platform

Likely project size and complexity:

- moderate to higher complexity than a standard website

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- portal or system options can be recommended after workflow discovery clarifies whether they are genuinely required

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting product-company scale or unlimited support coverage

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of portal usefulness, workflow improvements, or access-control outcomes

### Organisations relying heavily on spreadsheets, email, and duplicated data entry

Common problems:

- repeated manual handling
- errors from duplication
- unclear ownership of information
- long email approval chains and inconsistent handoffs

What they value:

- simplification
- consistency
- reduction of repetitive administration
- practical process modernisation without unnecessary system complexity

Likely project size and complexity:

- moderate operational-system work, sometimes phased over time

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- workflow and process improvement can surface missed self-service opportunities and staged changes before deeper implementation

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting a generic IT helpdesk or complete enterprise software replacement

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of workflow consolidation and reduced admin effort

### Clients needing a trusted technical partner rather than a one-off template website

Common problems:

- previous digital work lacked continuity
- no trusted adviser for incremental improvements
- confusion about what to prioritise next
- uncertainty about the real source of operational friction

What they value:

- judgement
- continuity
- calm communication
- someone who can work across website and deeper technical needs when appropriate
- an external practitioner who can identify overlooked improvement opportunities

Likely project size and complexity:

- variable, but often ongoing rather than strictly one-off

How Blackburn Studio can credibly help:

- the proposed model is a personal, led practice rather than a volume agency

Unsuitable client expectations:

- expecting instant staffing scale or fully managed multi-team delivery without limits

Useful evidence or case studies:

- examples of phased improvement, ongoing support, or trusted advisory relationships
