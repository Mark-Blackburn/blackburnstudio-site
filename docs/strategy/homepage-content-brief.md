# Homepage Content Brief

Status: Proposed

Last reviewed: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

Provide a clear launch content structure for `/` that keeps Blackburn Studio as the lead and gives equal pathway visibility to Photography and Digital.

Do not treat the homepage as a full-screen discipline chooser.

Do not write final website copy in this brief.

## Section 1: Blackburn Studio hero

Purpose:

- Lead with Blackburn Studio and introduce Photography and Digital in one coherent opening.

Audience question answered:

- "Who is Blackburn Studio, and what does the studio do?"

Required content:

- studio-led heading
- short positioning line referencing Photography and Digital
- restrained supporting intro

Primary CTA:

- Photography (`/work`)

Secondary CTA:

- Digital (`/digital`)

Visual role:

- immediate orientation and tone-setting entry section

Content risk:

- sounding like a generic agency or implying discipline parity means identical content volume

Evidence or assets required:

- approved positioning wording
- homepage visual treatment aligned with studio tone

## Section 2: Photography and Digital pathways

Purpose:

- Give clear side-by-side pathway choices without forcing a chooser-first experience.

Audience question answered:

- "Where should I go next for Photography versus Digital?"

Required content:

- short pathway summary for Photography
- short pathway summary for Digital
- explicit route destinations

Primary CTA:

- Photography (`/work`)

Secondary CTA:

- Digital (`/digital`)

Visual role:

- balanced pathway block beneath hero

Content risk:

- over-complex pathway language or ambiguous labels

Evidence or assets required:

- confirmed pathway labels and short descriptors

## Section 3: Selected Photography work

Purpose:

- Reinforce Photography as a complete, visible discipline through selected work previews.

Audience question answered:

- "What Photography work can I view right now?"

Required content:

- selected collection previews
- concise category descriptors
- route links for portraits, families, couples, and japan

Primary CTA:

- View all Photography (`/work`)

Secondary CTA:

- Direct collection links (`/work/portraits`, `/work/families`, `/work/couples`, `/work/japan`)

Visual role:

- image-led credibility and craft demonstration

Content risk:

- weak curation or mismatched category emphasis

Evidence or assets required:

- selected cover imagery and verified category metadata

Primary CTA:

- Explore Digital (`/digital`)

Secondary CTA:

- Contact (`/contact`)

Visual role:

- clear discipline expansion block with restrained hierarchy

Content risk:

Evidence or assets required:

## Section 5: Deeper Digital capability

Purpose:

- Present deeper capability with workflow and process improvement as the lead deeper capability, while keeping Websites as the simplest entry point.

Audience question answered:

- "Can Blackburn Studio identify operational friction and recommend practical improvements before choosing technology?"

Required content:

- workflow and process improvement as the lead deeper capability
- paper-to-digital modernisation
- identifying operational friction and duplicated handling
- platforms, portals, systems, and automation as possible solutions where suitable
- clear statement that Blackburn Studio first seeks to understand the problem
- boundaries and exclusions summary
- evidence-safe credibility statements

Possible content direction (not final copy):

- "Sometimes the problem is not the website. It is the paper form, repeated data entry, spreadsheet or email chain behind it."

Primary CTA:

- Discuss a Digital project (`/contact`)

Secondary CTA:

- Read About (`/about`)

Visual role:

- supporting capability layer below the websites-first introduction

Content risk:

- overclaiming capability without approved evidence
- positioning workflow support as enterprise consultancy rather than practical studio-led improvement

Evidence or assets required:

- approved capability boundaries
- any publishable supporting examples

## Section 8: Footer

Purpose:

- Provide consistent end-of-page navigation and utility links.

Audience question answered:

- "Where can I navigate next from here?"

Required content:

- footer links to Photography, Digital, About, Contact
- optional compliance links as required

Primary CTA:

- Photography (`/work`) or Digital (`/digital`) based on final visual order

Secondary CTA:

- About (`/about`) and Contact (`/contact`)

Visual role:

- structural close and navigation recovery layer

Content risk:

- footer links diverging from primary navigation targets

Evidence or assets required:

- final footer link set and legal/compliance link requirements

## Copy constraints

- Use restrained Australian English.
- Avoid inflated agency language and generic innovation claims.
- Keep wording concrete and verifiable.
- Keep Photography and Digital both visible in the final layout.
