## Purpose

Define the launch structure, messages, content hierarchy, project-highlight presentation, and conversion pathway for `/digital`.

This page should explain that Blackburn Studio provides practical Digital services covering:

1. Websites
2. Workflow and process improvement
3. Platforms, portals and business systems
4. Ongoing digital improvement

Websites remain the most accessible entry point.

Workflow and process improvement must be presented as a core capability, not a secondary technical service.

Technology should be presented as a response to the underlying need rather than the starting assumption.

Do not treat this brief as final public page copy.

## Page objectives

- make the Digital offer understandable to small-business and organisation leaders
- establish Websites as the accessible initial service
- show that Blackburn Studio can also solve deeper workflow and operational problems
- communicate discovery and proactive opportunity identification
- demonstrate credible delivered work
- distinguish commercial work from volunteer-community work
- avoid presenting Blackburn Studio as a large agency or enterprise consultancy
- encourage a conversation before prescribing a solution
- support future expansion into detailed project pages without requiring them at launch

## Primary audience

- small businesses
- trades and service businesses
- sporting organisations
- member organisations
- volunteer and community organisations
- businesses with outdated or fragmented websites
- organisations relying on paper, spreadsheets, email chains or knowledge held by individuals
- organisations needing portals, shared platforms or Microsoft 365 workflow improvement

## Core message

Internal message direction:

"Practical digital solutions shaped around how your business or organisation actually works."

Supporting ideas:

- understand the need before choosing the technology
- improve the process rather than simply digitising it
- build websites that support real business goals
- identify opportunities the original brief may not have considered
- deliver solutions that remain understandable and maintainable
- support continued improvement after launch

This is internal message direction, not final headline copy.
