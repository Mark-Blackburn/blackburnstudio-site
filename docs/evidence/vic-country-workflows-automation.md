## Workflow and process improvement

### Process before

- information distributed across personal inboxes
- Google Workspace and personal Google accounts in use
- compliance tracking performed through spreadsheets or not performed consistently
- paper forms in use
- repeated manual data entry
- manual reminders to clubs
- inconsistent document storage
- reports manually assembled or not available
- limited board visibility
- processes dependent on particular volunteers

### Additional opportunities identified

- Microsoft 365 migration
- role-based league and club accounts
- board and executive collaboration structures
- central governance and compliance document management
- structured club information
- form-based data collection
- compliance tracking
- automated reminders
- reporting
- per-club summaries
- board-level visibility
- PDF reporting
- automated email distribution
- sentiment analysis
- issue flagging
- due-date tracking
- easier volunteer handover
- broader process standardisation

### Improvement delivered

- spreadsheet and paper processes moved into structured SharePoint lists and Microsoft Forms where appropriate
- information moved from individual inboxes and informal storage into governed shared locations
- manual reminders were supplemented or replaced by automated notifications
- compliance status became visible centrally
- reports became available to the board and clubs through repeatable processes
- role-based accounts reduced dependence on specific individuals
- operating processes became easier to support and hand over

Do not claim complete elimination of manual work.
