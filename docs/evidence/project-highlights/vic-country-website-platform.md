## Client or organisational situation

- original request or starting point:
  - migrate Google-hosted email to Microsoft 365
  - assist with rolling out the supporting infrastructure
  - create simple SharePoint pages for individual clubs
- underlying problem:
  - the club pages were intended to be publicly accessible
  - SharePoint was not appropriate as the public website layer
  - individually maintained club pages would not provide a scalable or consistent solution
- additional opportunities identified by Mark Blackburn:
  - reusable website platform for all clubs
  - main league website on the same platform
  - centrally managed club and league content
  - PlayHQ-driven live competition information
  - scalable club onboarding
  - resilient integrations
  - calendar feeds
  - social-content integration
  - QR-code generation and analytics
  - observability and support tooling
  - future expansion without separate codebases per club

The shared website platform was conceived after the initial SharePoint-pages direction was found unsuitable and should be described that way.
